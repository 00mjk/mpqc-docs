mpqc::math::PetiteList Class Referenceabstract

Documentation

A helper class for symmetric tensor algebra.

PetiteList implements auxiliary representation theory algebra needed to evaluate efficiently expressions involving tensors with symmetries (permutation and otherwise). The concept was introduced by Michel Dupuis and Harry King (DOI 10.1002/qua.560110408).

Note
only permutation groups are supported right now
